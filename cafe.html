Two Coffees

Two origins. One table.

Italian Roast

Lavazza

More than a century of Italian roasting behind every pour. Lavazza delivers the dense crema, the aromatic depth, and the clean, lingering finish that makes an espresso feel like a small ceremony. The foundation of our espressos, lattes, and cappuccinos.

Bolivian Highland Coffee

Jaqaku

From Bolivia's high valleys, where thin air and volcanic soil conspire to produce something unexpected — a cup that is bright without being sharp, complex without demanding study. Jaqaku tastes like where it comes from. That is the point.
