Our Story

A café born from
a love for Sucre

Me Gusta Café is the morning heartbeat of our little ecosystem on Bolivar #603. We opened because we believe good coffee and good conversation belong at the center of every traveler's day in Sucre.

We source our beans directly from Bolivian producers in the yungas and high valleys — some of the most underrated coffee-growing regions in the world. Every cup is roasted locally and brewed with care.

Lo que servimos

El café que elegimos

Cada taza que servimos viene de dos fuentes que creemos en ellas — una con historia mundial, la otra con raíces bolivianas.

Café Italiano

Lavazza

Una de las marcas de café más reconocidas del mundo. Blend italiano de carácter intenso, crema perfecta y aroma que llena el salón. Lo usamos para nuestros espressos, lattes y cappuccinos.

Café Boliviano

Jaqaku

Granos bolivianos de altura con identidad propia. Jaqaku trae los sabores únicos de los valles de Bolivia — suave, aromático y con el carácter de nuestra tierra.

The Space

Your quiet corner
in the White City

Our café occupies a colonial patio on Bolivar #603 — high ceilings, arched windows, and natural light in the morning. The kind of place where an hour turns into three.

Free WiFi, power outlets at every table, and staff that knows Sucre inside out. Whether you're planning your day, catching up on work, or just reading — you're welcome here.
